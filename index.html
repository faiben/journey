The Journey

A story about courage and collaboration

An epic animated adventure featuring six children from different cultures united by a common goal to save their parents.

About The Project

A Bundle of Adventure

The project is a unique bundle containing a high-quality animated feature film and an immersive video game that continues the story.

Set in the late 15th century, this cross-cultural adventure follows six children from different backgrounds who must work together to overcome incredible challenges and rescue their parents from a ruthless pirate.

With universal themes of courage, collaboration, and the unconditional love between children and their parents, this production is designed to resonate with audiences worldwide.

The Video Game

Continue The Adventure

The video game extends the film's narrative, allowing players to experience the adventure interactively.

Players can choose to play as any of the six main characters, each with unique abilities that must be used strategically to overcome challenges.

The game features puzzle-solving, exploration, and action elements, set in beautifully rendered environments from the film.

Project Objectives

Creating an Ethical Alternative

The main objective of the project is the creation of an ethical alternative in film and video game production that promotes positive values and cultural understanding.

Establish Characters

Create beloved characters from diverse backgrounds that can be used for future productions, building a lasting franchise.

Stimulate Talent

Develop local talents across all production areas including animation, writing, directing, and marketing.

Distribution Channels

Establish local distribution channels to ensure the project reaches its intended audiences effectively.
